Dreamcatcher (드림캐쳐) is a seven-member girl group under Dreamcatcher Company. They debuted on January 13, 2017 with the single "Nightmare". With strong rock and metal influence Dreamcatcher is known as the face of rock in K-pop. Name of the fandom: InSomnia.
